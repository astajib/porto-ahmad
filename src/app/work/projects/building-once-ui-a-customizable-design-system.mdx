## Overview

Odoo implementation is the process of deploying Odoo, an all-in-one business management software, to streamline and automate business processes. The implementation typically follows a structured approach to ensure successful adoption and minimal disruption.

## Key Features

- **CRM Module**: Odoo CRM is the true customer-centric tool for your sales needs. Track leads, get accurate forecasts, and focus on what matters: closing opportunities.
- **Sales Module**: The Sales module in Odoo automates and streamlines the sales cycle from quotation to order and invoicing.
- **Inventory Module**: Inventory module is a powerful tool designed to efficiently manage stock levels, warehouse operations, and supply chain logistics.
- **Website Module**: Build company profile website and integration with certificate module.
- **Certification Module**: Developed certification module and validation rule for their client.
